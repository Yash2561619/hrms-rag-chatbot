1. LEAVE POLICY
===============================================================================

1.1 Types of Leave

Employees of Next AI Technologies are entitled to the following leave:

1.1.1 Casual Leave
- Entitlement: 12 days per calendar year
- Application: Must apply at least 2 days in advance
- Carry-forward: Unused casual leave cannot be carried forward
- Restrictions: Cannot take more than 3 consecutive days without manager approval

1.1.2 Sick Leave
- Entitlement: 10 days per calendar year
- Application: Can be taken without advance notice
- Reporting: Must report to manager within 24 hours of taking leave
- Documentation: Medical certificate required for leaves exceeding 3 consecutive days
- Carry-forward: Up to 5 days can be carried forward to next year

1.1.3 Annual Leave
- Entitlement: 20 days per calendar year
- Application: Must apply with at least 2 weeks notice
- Manager Approval: Requires manager and HOD approval
- Blackout Periods: Cannot take during product launches or critical deliverables
- Carry-forward: Maximum 10 days can be carried forward to next year

1.1.4 Bereavement Leave
- Entitlement: 3 days for immediate family (parents, spouse, children)
- Entitlement: 1 day for extended family (grandparents, siblings)
- Documentation: Death certificate or obituary notice required

1.2 Leave Application Process

Step 1: Submit application through the HR portal at least 2 days in advance
Step 2: Receive approval notification via email
Step 3: Ensure work handover is complete before leave
Step 4: Provide leave report link to manager
Step 5: On return, complete "Return to Work" checklist

1.3 Leave Encashment

- Unused leave at year-end is forfeited and cannot be carried forward
- Exception: Earned leave can be encashed at separation at basic salary rate
- Encashment amount: (unused days × daily rate × basic salary) ÷ 365
- No partial day encashment; minimum encashment unit is 1 full day

1.4 Unauthorized Absence

- Taking leave without approval results in loss of pay
- Repeated unauthorized absence (3+ instances) may result in termination
- Absence during critical periods attracts additional disciplinary action

2. SALARY AND BENEFITS
===============================================================================

2.1 Salary Processing

- Processing Date: 25th of every month
- Payment Method: Direct transfer to employee's registered bank account
- Delayed Processing: In case of banking issues, HR will notify within 24 hours
- Holidays: If 25th falls on a weekend/holiday, payment is made on the previous working day

2.2 Salary Structure

Monthly salary comprises:
- Basic Salary: Fixed base compensation
- House Rent Allowance (HRA): Location-based allowance
- Dearness Allowance (DA): Inflation adjustment
- Special Allowance: Additional compensation
- Variable Pay: Performance-linked bonus (if applicable)

2.3 Salary Slip

- Availability: Digital salary slips generated on 25th of every month
- Access: Available in employee portal under "Payroll" section
- Official Record: Digital salary slip is the official and legal record
- Retention: Employees must retain slips for 3 years for tax and compliance purposes

2.4 Tax Deductions

- Income Tax: As per government rules (TDS deducted monthly)
- Professional Tax: Applicable as per state regulations
- Employee Provident Fund (PF): 12% of basic salary
- Employee State Insurance (ESI): As per government mandate

2.5 Performance Bonus

- Eligibility: Employees with 6+ months tenure
- Basis: Performance rating and company revenue targets
- Payout: Typically in December
- Calculation: 0-100% of monthly basic salary (varies by rating)

3. HEALTH INSURANCE
===============================================================================

3.1 Coverage

- Scope: All employees are automatically covered from day 1 of employment
- Plan Type: Comprehensive health insurance with cashless hospitalization
- Coverage Amount: Rs. 5,00,000 per employee per year
- Family Plan: Spouse and dependent children can be added

3.2 Eligible Dependents

- Spouse: Legally married spouse
- Children: Biological/adopted children up to age 23 (or 25 if pursuing education)
- Parents: Parents of employees above age 60 (additional premium)

3.3 Health Insurance Claims

3.3.1 In-Network Hospitals
- Coverage: 100% cashless treatment at empaneled hospitals
- Listing: Hospital network available on the insurer's website
- Direct Settlement: Hospital settles claim directly with insurer

3.3.2 Out-of-Network Claims
- Reimbursement: 80% of actual expense (20% employee co-pay)
- Documentation: Original receipts, prescriptions, medical reports required
- Submission Deadline: Claims must be submitted within 30 days of incurring expense
- Processing Time: Claims processed within 7-10 business days
- Late Claims: Claims submitted after 30 days are rejected

3.3.3 Required Documentation
- Original itemized bill/receipt from hospital
- Prescription from treating physician
- Medical reports and test results (if any)
- Insurance claim form (signed and dated)
- Proof of identity

3.4 Annual Medical Check-up

- Frequency: Once per calendar year
- Coverage: 100% employer-paid
- Scope: Basic health screening, blood work, ECG, etc.
- Scheduling: Through HR department

4. IT SECURITY POLICY
===============================================================================

4.1 Password Policy

Requirement: All passwords must adhere to the following standards:
- Minimum Length: 12 characters
- Character Mix: Must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
- Expiry: Password must be changed every 90 days
- Reusability: Cannot reuse the same password within last 5 changes
- Sharing: Passwords must not be shared with anyone, including IT staff

4.2 Data Protection

- Confidential Data: All company documents are confidential
- USB Devices: Personal USB devices are not allowed
- Cloud Storage: Only approved cloud services (Google Drive, OneDrive) can be used
- Backup: All work must be saved to company servers, not personal devices
- Encryption: All laptops must use full-disk encryption

4.3 Incident Reporting

- Data Breach: Report immediately to IT@nextai.tech
- Suspicious Activity: Report phishing emails or unusual access attempts
- Lost Device: Report lost/stolen laptops or phones within 1 hour
- Response Time: IT responds to security incidents within 2 hours

5. REMOTE WORK POLICY
===============================================================================

5.1 Eligibility

- Tenure: Minimum 6 months of employment
- Role: Must be approved by department head
- Performance: Performance rating must be "Meets Expectations" or above

5.2 Remote Work Allowance

- Days per Week: Up to 3 days per week (Monday-Friday)
- Scheduling: Must plan with manager by end of previous week
- Equipment: Company provides laptop and Internet allowance (Rs. 1,000/month)
- Workspace: Employee responsible for adequate workspace at home

5.3 Attendance Tracking

- Logging: Must log "Remote" status in HRMS before start of day
- Time Tracking: Work hours tracked via activity monitoring tool
- Availability: Must be available on company communication channels
- Meetings: Attend all scheduled meetings, whether in-office or virtual

5.4 Conduct

- Confidentiality: No discussing confidential work in public places
- Distractions: Minimize background noise during video calls
- Professional Appearance: Dress appropriately for video meetings
- Workspace: Ensure professional background for video calls

6. CODE OF CONDUCT
===============================================================================

6.1 Professional Behavior

- Respect: Treat all colleagues with respect and dignity
- Harassment: Zero tolerance for sexual harassment, discrimination, bullying
- Conduct: Maintain professional demeanor in all interactions
- Dress Code: Business casual dress code (Monday-Friday, in-office)

6.2 Conflict of Interest

- Outside Work: Prior written approval required for outside employment
- Gifts: Cannot accept gifts exceeding Rs. 5,000 in value
- Relatives: Cannot directly or indirectly report to a family member
- Vendor Relations: Cannot have financial relationships with vendors

6.3 Compliance

- Violations: Any violation is grounds for disciplinary action
- Escalation: Violations reported to Ethics@nextai.tech are investigated independently
- Confidentiality: Reporter identity protected during investigation
- Action: Violations may result in suspension, termination, or legal action

7. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT
===============================================================================

7.1 Performance Appraisal Cycle

- Frequency: Annual appraisal (January)
- Mid-year: Informal check-in with manager (July)
- Ratings: 1 (Below) to 5 (Exceptional)
- Timelines: Appraisals completed by end of January

7.2 Rating Scale

1. Below Expectations: Does not meet minimum job requirements
2. Needs Development: Meets some but not all requirements
3. Meets Expectations: Meets all job requirements consistently
4. Exceeds Expectations: Consistently goes beyond requirements
5. Exceptional: Top performer, role model for organization

7.3 Career Development

- Learning Budget: Rs. 50,000 per employee per year
- Courses: Prior approval required for online courses
- Certifications: Company reimburses 100% of job-related certification costs
- Mentorship: Matched with senior mentors for career guidance
